We're Business Solutions. We Kill the Other Kind.

We launched Veritas BS because we're tired of watching projects fail when someone on the team saw it coming months earlier.

Our Mission

To prevent project disasters through organised honesty before it's too late.

We believe most project failures are predictable. Someone on the team usually knows by week 8 that things aren't working. They just don't feel safe saying it.

The Reality Check methodology creates a safe space for uncomfortable truths to surface before they become expensive disasters.

Our Values

Honesty Over Comfort

We believe uncomfortable truths are better than comfortable lies.

Projects fail when teams avoid difficult conversations. We create safe spaces for honest dialogue.

Evidence Over Opinion

We demand proof, not assumptions.

Customer quotes, usage data, money paid. Not research, not opinions, not "we think."

Learning Over Ego

We embrace surprises as learning opportunities.

If nothing surprises you, you're not learning. Learning prevents disasters.

Why We Started

We've seen too many projects fail the same way:

  • Someone on the team knew it would fail by week 8
  • They didn't feel safe speaking up
  • The project continued for months
  • Millions were wasted
  • Everyone pretended they didn't see it coming

We're launching The Reality Check methodology because we believe this pattern can be broken with three simple questions, asked weekly, in a safe environment.
